Interview

发表于 2016-09-21   |   分类于 daily   |  

We’re happy and extremely excited when we receive a interview notice. But also many guys have a little bit nervous when they have a talk with the interviewer face to face. It’s very common for every one. Because when we in a new circumstance, everything looks strange and feel a little or a lot uneasy. For the old driver it’s nothing. They are not afraid of it. They have a lots of experiences at interviewing. It’s a piece of cake for them. But for the new guys who is just graduated from college is a little bit hard.

But if we prepare enough, it will’t be a problem. First, we should know the jobs’s detail and what’s the most important skills it needed. Second, understand what’s the interviewer want to know from you. Third, take a test yourself to demo interview flow. You can let your friend act as the interviewer to preview it. Every interviewer will start the interview from these very basic information about you.

Also we may have a small chat to exchange greetings. Just like : Thank you for having us or Thank you for inviting us. And if you bring some gifts to your friends. You can say: Here’s a little something for you.
To reply this, we could say: You shouldn’t have.

If you want to express your opinion or thinking about your friends house decoration. We always say I love what you have done in the place. But if it’s not done by yourself, you maybe say I can’t take credit for it though. It was all my wife , husband , girl friend or boy friend.
