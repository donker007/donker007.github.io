Interview

发表于 2016-09-21   |   分类于 daily   |  

We’re happy and extremely excited when we receive a interview notice. But also many guys have a little bit nervous when they have a talk with the interviewer face to face. It’s very common for every one. Because when we in a new circumstance, everything looks strange and feel a little or a lot uneasy. For the old driver it’s nothing. They are not afraid of it. They have a lots of experiences at interviewing. It’s a piece of cake for them. But for the new guys who is just graduated from college is a little bit hard.

But if we prepare enough, it will’t be a problem. First, we should know the jobs’s detail and what’s the most important skills it needed. Second, understand what’s the interviewer want to know from you. Third, take a test yourself to demo interview flow. You can let your friend act as the interviewer to preview it. So it can help you familiar with interview flow to establish confidence. Also we should prepare some words to talk about your background,education and work experiences. Every interviewer will start the interview from these very basic information about you.

Neighbor is very import for us in china. When we’re children we live around at country. We have many little friends and play together, we know each other like brother or sister. But nowadays. It seems to be a great different. Everything is changing so fast and neighbors are changed with the same speed. Many of

We can’t go back. Of course we can’t change the trend of society but to integrate into society.
